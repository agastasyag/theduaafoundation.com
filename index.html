Who We Are

The Duaa Foundation is a registered non-profit that partners with community schools to deliver quality education resources, after-school tutoring and scholarship aid. Founded in 2012, we have reached more than 12,000 learners in rural and urban centres.

Our Programs

School Supplies Drive

Annual distribution of notebooks, uniforms and tablets to equip children for effective learning.

Peer Mentoring

Trained volunteer mentors guide students in goal-setting, study skills and building self-confidence.

Scholarship Fund

Need-based tuition awards open the door to higher education for promising youth from low-income families.
